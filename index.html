Get help with your timetable

General

The below includes common issues and ways to fix them, if you still have a problem after trying the below fixes please contact me.



Pro Version

After purchasing the Pro Version if you have any issues please follow the below.

  • Make sure you are connected to wifi when purchasing.
  • Make sure you actually purchased the Pro Version, try restoring the purchase.
  • Close the app fully (double click and remove from history) and then go back into it. Once again try restoring the purchase. If the purchase won't restore, you haven't made the purchase, so try buying it again.
  • If you have successfully purchased the Pro Version and not unlocked any of the features please get in touch after trying the above steps.

The Widget

After purchasing the Pro Version, you can setup the widget. If you recieve a red X next to Class Times try the below to fix it.

  • Ensure you have setup class times correctly.
  • - Make sure no times overlap eachother
  • - Make sure that your am and pm starts and finishes are correct.
  • Ensure every period for every day has a start and finish time.
  • Ensure your class times are in order, do not have a class starting at 5:00pm followed by a class starting at 4:00pm

After purchasing the Pro Version, you can setup the widget. If you recieve a red X next to Setup Timetable try the below to fix it.

  • Ensure you have setup your timetable (you should have a set number of days and periods), if you are having issues, try viewing the tutorial again.

After purchasing the Pro Version, you can setup the widget. If it does not load try the following.

  • Remove the widget from your widgets, then add it again.

After purchasing the Pro Version, you can setup the widget. If the class overlay, which tells you how far through a class you are is not functioning correctly or any other general issues:

  • Remove the widget from your widgets, then add it again.
  • Ensure the class times are set correctly.

Apple Watch

After purchasing the Pro Version, you can sync your Apple Watch. If you are having trouble with it, try the steps below.

  • Ensure you have setup your timetable, if you are having issues, try viewing the tutorial again.
  • Open the app on both your phone and watch, make sure they are connected and then press sync.
  • You must manually sync whenever you update your timetable.

Exporting to Calendar

After purchasing the Pro Version, you can export your timetable to your calendar. If you are having any issues try the below:

  • Ensure you have setup your timetable, if you are having issues, try viewing the tutorial again.
  • Make sure all your classes have a start and finish time.
  • Make sure your classes have names.

General information about exporting:

  • Exporting will create a new iCloud calendar called Timetable Plus
  • It will export your timetable to your calendar. It will export your timetable (however many weeks you have) 5 times. For example a 2 week timetable will go for 10 weeks, a 3 week calendar for 15.
  • You can delete your calendar and all the events easily from the calendar app, by selecting caledars and remove calendar.
  • Each time you export it will re-create the events.
  • Exporting requires iCloud and accepting the calendar access request, otherwise no events can be created.
